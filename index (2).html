Current Projects

New Greenhouse Construction

Started: March 2025

We're building a 30x60 foot greenhouse to extend our growing season and provide fresh vegetables year-round. The structure will feature rainwater collection and solar heating. Expected completion: August 2025.

Heritage Breed Chicken Expansion

Started: February 2025

Adding 50 heritage breed chickens to our flock, including Rhode Island Reds and Buff Orpingtons. This will increase our egg production while preserving these wonderful traditional breeds.

Medicinal Herb Garden

Started: April 2025

Planting a dedicated medicinal herb garden featuring chamomile, echinacea, lavender, and other healing plants. These will be used in our natural skincare products and herbal teas.

About Our Homestead

Welcome to The Blessed Homestead, where tradition meets sustainability in the heart of the countryside. Our journey began five years ago when we decided to leave the hustle and bustle of city life to pursue a simpler, more meaningful way of living.

Our 40-acre homestead is home to a diverse array of animals including dairy cows, goats, chickens, and ducks. We practice regenerative agriculture, working with nature to create a thriving ecosystem that benefits both our animals and the land.

Every product we offer is crafted with care, using traditional methods passed down through generations. From our artisan cheeses aged in our own cheese cave to our handmade soaps infused with herbs from our garden, we believe in providing our community with pure, wholesome products.

We're not just farmers – we're stewards of the land, committed to sustainable practices that will preserve this beautiful homestead for future generations. Thank you for supporting our journey and allowing us to share the fruits of our labor with your family.
